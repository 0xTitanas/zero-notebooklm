Usage: notebooklm research status [OPTIONS]

  Check research status for the current notebook.

  Shows whether research is in progress, completed, or not running.

  Examples:
    notebooklm research status
    notebooklm research status --json

Options:
  -n, --notebook TEXT  Notebook ID (uses current if not set). Supports partial
                       IDs.  [env var: NOTEBOOKLM_NOTEBOOK]
  --json               Output as JSON
  --help               Show this message and exit.
