Usage: notebooklm share remove [OPTIONS] EMAIL

  Remove a user's access to the notebook.

  Examples:
    notebooklm share remove user@example.com
    notebooklm share remove user@example.com -y  # Skip confirmation

Options:
  -n, --notebook TEXT  Notebook ID (uses current if not set). Supports partial
                       IDs.  [env var: NOTEBOOKLM_NOTEBOOK]
  -y, --yes            Skip confirmation
  --json               Output as JSON
  --help               Show this message and exit.
